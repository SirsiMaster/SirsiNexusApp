## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process personal data only when we have a valid legal basis:

| Legal Basis | Description | Examples |
|-------------|-------------|----------|
| **Consent (Art. 6(1)(a))** | You have given clear consent for specific purposes | Marketing emails, cookies, special category data |
| **Contract (Art. 6(1)(b))** | Processing is necessary for contract performance | Account management, service delivery, payments |
| **Legal Obligation (Art. 6(1)(c))** | Processing required by law | Tax records, AML compliance, court orders |
| **Vital Interests (Art. 6(1)(d))** | Processing necessary to protect life | Emergency contact notification |
| **Legitimate Interests (Art. 6(1)(f))** | Our legitimate business interests | Security, fraud prevention, service improvement |

### 4.1 Consent Management

- Consent is freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous
- Consent can be withdrawn at any time without affecting lawfulness of prior processing
- Consent records are maintained with timestamp and scope
- Separate consent is obtained for each distinct processing purpose
